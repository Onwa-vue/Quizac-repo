Frequently asked Questions

What are Question Sets?

Question sets are bundles of questions around a given topic playable on the quizac app.

Who can sign up as a contributor?

Anyone with a verifiable knowledge in academic subject areas, who is also interested in putting the effort to create the questions can sign up as a content contributor.

What do I need to become a contributor?

Every contributor is required to sign up, complete their contributor profile and get verified.

What are the acceptable content formats?

For now, only image (jpeg, png or gifs) and texts content formats are acceptable. More formats will be added soon.

How do I publish and get paid for my content?

You can publish your content as a set of questions which conforms to our content contribution guide. After publishing your content, It becomes available to students on the Quizac app, and you get paid when a student makes a purchase.
